For your social media app, before writing any new endpoint, spend 2 minutes writing:

Endpoint:
Authentication:
Input:
Validation:
Business Rules:
Database Operations:
Response:

For example:

Endpoint: Like Post

Authentication: Required

Input:
- postId

Validation:
- post exists

Business Rules:
- user can only like once

Database:
- add user to likes

Response:
- success
